Biography

Yinghao MA (马英浩) is a senior student of School of Mathematical Science, PKU, majoring Mathematics and Probability Theory. His research interests include Music Information Retrieval, Audio Signal Process, Machine Learning and Probability Theory. His goal is to deepen human understanding of auditory signals with information technology, especially music.

Besides, he is one of the student conductors of Chinese philharmonic Orchestra, Chinese Music Institute at PKU (see at Facebook). He is also an advocate of affirmative action, carrying out public welfare activities at college student union.

He is now applying for Graduate research, in pursuit of his passion about exploring new things and innovating new ideas.
